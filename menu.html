About

La Degustación is a restaurant that combines tradition and innovation, offering a unique and personalized gastronomic experience. Its commitment to quality, sustainability and passion for cooking have made it a benchmark in Constanza, positioning it as the gastronomic destination of choice for those looking to enjoy the best flavors of Spain and the Dominican Republic.
